Guide: Discount Calculator

What it does

Computes reduced price after a percentage discount.

How to use it

Enter original price and discount percentage, then calculate.

Formula

Sale = Original × (1 − d%); Savings = Original − Sale.

Example

$120 item at 25% off → $90 final price, $30 saved.

FAQs

Are two 20% discounts equal to 40% off?

No. Sequential percents multiply: 0.8×0.8 = 36% off from original.

Can I enter percent as 15 instead of 0.15?

Use the form as labeled—typically enter 15 for 15%.

Does this include shipping?

Only if you include shipping in the base amount you tax.
