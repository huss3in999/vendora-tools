Guide: Pythagorean Theorem

What it does

Solves for hypotenuse or a leg depending on inputs.

How to use it

Enter two known sides; leave the third blank or use the mode provided.

Formula

c = √(a² + b²) for hypotenuse c.

Example

Legs 6 and 8 → hypotenuse 10.

FAQs

What if the triangle isn’t right?

Use law of cosines instead; this tool assumes 90°.

Which side is c?

c is the hypotenuse opposite the right angle.

Integer triangles?

Pythagorean triples like 3-4-5 satisfy the relation exactly.
