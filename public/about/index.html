Vendora Platform

About Vendora

Vendora is built for restaurants and small food businesses that need simple operational tools to manage pricing, ordering, invoicing, and daily restaurant workflows without unnecessary complexity.

What Vendora Does

Vendora is focused on practical restaurant software and free tools that help owners make better day-to-day decisions.

Restaurant management system

Vendora is moving toward a restaurant management system that connects POS and operations with simpler day-to-day tools that restaurant owners can actually use.

Tools that solve real tasks

The platform already includes a QR menu generator, invoice generator, food cost calculator, and menu price calculator to support pricing and service flow.

Who Vendora Is Built For

Vendora stays focused on food businesses instead of trying to be a general tool site for every industry.

  • Small restaurants that need clearer pricing, ordering, and daily operations support.
  • Cafes that want practical tools for menus, invoices, and smoother service workflows.
  • Food trucks and takeaway shops that need simple tools they can use quickly.
  • Cloud kitchens and catering businesses that rely on efficient pricing and order handling.
  • Bakeries and juice shops that want restaurant-style tools without extra complexity.

You can explore the current tool set in the Vendora tools hub, review the focused restaurant calculators page, or return to the homepage for the broader platform view.

Why Vendora Focuses on Restaurants

Restaurant operations are different from generic business workflows, so Vendora keeps the product direction centered on restaurant owners and food service teams.

Restaurant-specific decisions

Food cost, menu pricing, QR menus, invoice handling, and POS workflows all need restaurant-first wording and logic, not generic business templates.

Simple operational tools

Vendora aims to keep pricing, operations, and customer-facing tools practical for operators who need answers quickly during a busy day.
