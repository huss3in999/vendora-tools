Guide: Speed, Distance, Time

What it does

Rearranges v = d/t to find the missing variable.

How to use it

Enter any two of distance, speed, time; leave the third empty.

Formula

v = d/t; d = v×t; t = d/v.

Example

120 km in 2 h → 60 km/h.

FAQs

Do units need to match?

Yes—use consistent pairs (miles with hours, km with hours).

Average vs instantaneous speed?

This model assumes constant speed over the interval.

Metric vs imperial?

Convert to one system before entering; don’t mix miles with km without converting.
