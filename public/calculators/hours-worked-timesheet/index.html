Guide: Hours Worked / Timesheet

What it does

Adds time entries and compares to a 40-hour threshold.

How to use it

Enter hours or clock ranges per weekday as the form allows.

Formula

Weekly total = sum of daily hours; OT = max(0, total − 40) if using 40h rule.

Example

8+8+8+8+10 = 42 → 2 overtime hours under a simple rule.

FAQs

Does this follow labor law?

No. Payroll law varies by jurisdiction and contract.

Daily overtime?

Some regions require OT after 8h/day; this model uses a weekly 40h example only.

Decimal hours?

Enter 7.5 for 7h30m if the form expects decimals.
