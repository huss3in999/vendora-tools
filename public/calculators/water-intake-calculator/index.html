Guide: Water Intake Calculator

What it does

Provides a simple daily fluid intake target based on weight and a light activity adjustment.

How to use it

Enter weight (kg) and an activity level, then calculate.

Formula

Uses a baseline per-kg guideline plus a multiplier or add-on for higher activity.

Example

70 kg with moderate activity might suggest a few liters per day including water from food—interpret as a rough target.

FAQs

Do coffee and tea count?

Most fluids contribute to hydration; alcohol can dehydrate. This tool estimates total beverage needs broadly.

What if I exercise heavily?

Add extra fluids around workouts and replace electrolytes for long sessions.

Are there kidney or heart conditions?

Fluid limits may apply. This calculator is not medical advice.
