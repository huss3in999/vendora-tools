Guide: Force Calculator

What it does

Chained SI calculations for Newtons and Pascals.

How to use it

Enter mass, acceleration, and area.

Formula

F = m×a; P = F/A.

Example

10 kg × 9.8 m/s² = 98 N over 2 m² → 49 Pa.

FAQs

Is g exactly 9.8?

Varies slightly by location; adjust a if needed.

psi vs Pa?

1 psi ≈ 6895 Pa; convert if you need imperial pressure.

Gauge vs absolute pressure?

This model uses absolute force/area unless you offset atmospheric pressure manually.
