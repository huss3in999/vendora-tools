Guide: Density Calculator

What it does

Divides mass by volume.

How to use it

Enter mass and volume, then calculate.

Formula

ρ = m/V.

Example

100 g in 50 cm³ → 2 g/cm³.

FAQs

Other units?

Convert to consistent mass/volume units before entering.

Porosity?

Bulk volume includes voids; material density may differ from bulk density.

Liquids?

Same formula; measure mass and contained volume carefully.
