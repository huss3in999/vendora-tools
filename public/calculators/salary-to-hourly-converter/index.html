Guide: Salary to Hourly Converter

What it does

Divides yearly pay by total annual hours.

How to use it

Enter annual salary, hours per week, and weeks worked per year, then calculate.

Formula

Hourly ≈ Annual ÷ (hours per week × weeks per year).

Example

$60,000/year at 40 h/week, 50 weeks → $30/hour before taxes.

FAQs

Why 52 vs 50 weeks?

Adjust for unpaid leave or holidays if your schedule differs.

Is this my take-home pay?

No. It is gross rate before tax withholding and benefits.

What about overtime?

This model assumes a flat annual salary; overtime rules vary by employer.
