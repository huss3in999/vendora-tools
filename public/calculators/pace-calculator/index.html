Guide: Pace Calculator

What it does

Divides total minutes by distance to get pace.

How to use it

Enter distance in km and total time in minutes.

Formula

Pace (min/km) = total minutes ÷ distance (km).

Example

5 km in 25 min → 5:00 per km average.

FAQs

How do I convert to min/mile?

Multiply min/km by 1.60934, or use a dedicated converter.

Hill courses?

Pace varies by terrain; this is overall average pace.

Treadmill km vs outdoor?

Same math if distance and time are accurate.
