Guide: Blood Alcohol Content (BAC)

What it does

Approximates BAC using a simplified ethanol distribution model.

How to use it

Enter number of standard drinks, body weight, sex, and time since first drink.

Formula

Widmark-style: alcohol mass divided by body water volume, adjusted by metabolism over time (simplified constants).

Example

Two standard drinks in one hour may produce a different BAC than the same drinks spread over three hours.

FAQs

Can I use this to decide if I can drive?

No. Never rely on this for legal decisions. Use a certified breathalyzer and follow local laws.

Why do estimates vary?

Food, medications, tolerance, and health affect BAC; models are averages only.

What is a standard drink?

Roughly 14 g ethanol in US definitions (e.g., ~12 oz beer, 5 oz wine, 1.5 oz spirits).
