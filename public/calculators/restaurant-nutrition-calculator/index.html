Restaurant nutrition calculator

High-traffic searches like “Chipotle nutrition calculator” expect live chain menus. Those databases require constant refresh, regional variants, and clear data provenance. This release gives you a transparent manual basket: type calories (and optional macros) from the restaurant’s own nutrition PDF or on-site tool, and we sum them deterministically—no mystery scraping.

Trust checklist (copy the pattern major chains use)

  • State whether values come from accredited-lab testing, published tables, or supplier data.
  • Explain standard portions vs real-line variation (dressings, soda ice, well-done trims).
  • Show last-reviewed date when you maintain a database.
  • Round per FDA-style rules so in-app sums may differ slightly from exact arithmetic.

In the United States, large chains already participate in a disclosure ecosystem (menu calorie labels and written nutrition). Partner APIs such as Nutritionix or direct ingestion of official PDFs are the sustainable engineering path—planned as a follow-on once a refresh workflow exists.
