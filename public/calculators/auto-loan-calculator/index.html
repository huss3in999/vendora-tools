Guide: Auto Loan Calculator

What it does

Computes amortizing payment on the financed amount.

How to use it

Enter price, down payment, APR, and months, then calculate.

Formula

Same amortization as mortgages with monthly compounding of interest.

Example

Finance $25,000 at 7% for 60 months → roughly $495/month before fees.

FAQs

Should I include sales tax?

If you roll tax into the loan, include it in amount financed.

What about 0% APR deals?

Divide principal by months if truly zero finance charge; verify fine print.

Does this include gap insurance?

No—add any financed add-ons to principal if applicable.
