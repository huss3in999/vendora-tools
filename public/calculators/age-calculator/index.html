Guide: Age Calculator

What it does

Computes calendar difference between two dates.

How to use it

Enter birth date and “as of” date, then calculate.

Formula

Calendar arithmetic across years, months, and days (implementation-specific rounding).

Example

Born 2000-01-01, as of 2026-01-01 → 26 years.

FAQs

Does it account for leap years?

Date math should; verify edge cases around Feb 29.

Time zones?

Dates without times use calendar dates in local time—be careful near midnight UTC.

Legal age vs calculated age?

Jurisdictions define rules differently; this is a general calculator.
