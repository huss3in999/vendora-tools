Guide: Kinetic Energy

What it does

Evaluates ½mv².

How to use it

Enter mass and velocity, then calculate.

Formula

KE = ½ m v².

Example

10 kg at 5 m/s → 125 J.

FAQs

Relativistic speeds?

Classical formula breaks down near light speed.

km/h instead of m/s?

Convert to m/s before entering: m/s = km/h ÷ 3.6.

Rotational kinetic energy?

That uses moment of inertia; this tool is linear KE only.
