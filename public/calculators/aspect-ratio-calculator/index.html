Guide: Aspect Ratio

What it does

Maintains W:H while solving for one dimension.

How to use it

Enter original W×H and either new width or new height (other zero).

Formula

W2/H2 = W1/H1 → cross-multiply for missing side.

Example

1920×1080 scaled to width 1280 → height 720.

FAQs

Pixel-perfect streaming?

Codecs may require even dimensions; round accordingly.

Letterboxing?

This tool scales proportionally; black bars are added separately in players.

Anamorphic video?

Use display aspect after de-squeeze; pixel aspect may differ.
