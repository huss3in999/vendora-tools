Classic rule vs real life

The old “3,500 kcal per pound” shortcut often overpredicts how fast weight changes. Dynamic models (for example the NIH Body Weight Planner) account for adaptive metabolism. Our goal-date line uses a simple energy-density band so you can compare your chosen deficit with the calendar—not replace clinical planning.
