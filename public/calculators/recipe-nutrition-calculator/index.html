Recipe nutrition calculator

Paste values from packaging or the USDA FoodData Central “per 100 g” view. For each row: edible grams you use, then nutrients per 100 g of that ingredient. We multiply, sum, optionally scale by cooked yield % (water loss), then divide by servings. Sodium and sugar fields are optional extras aligned with Nutrition Facts-style thinking.

Net carbs are not FDA-defined; this tool shows total carbohydrate first. If you subtract fibre manually, label that in your own notes.
