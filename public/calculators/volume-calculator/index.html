FAQs

Are these exact for manufacturing?

Mathematically exact from inputs; real objects have tolerances.

Cylinder: radius or diameter?

Confirm whether the form expects radius—doubling halves volume if misread.

Units?

Volume is in cubic length units consistent with your inputs.
