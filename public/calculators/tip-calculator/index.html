Guide: Tip Calculator

What it does

Splits checks with tip and optional rounding.

How to use it

Enter subtotal, tip percent, and number of people, then calculate.

Formula

Tip = subtotal × tip%; total = subtotal + tip; per person = total ÷ n.

Example

$80 bill, 18% tip, 4 people → $14.40 tip, $23.60 each.

FAQs

Tip on pre-tax or post-tax?

Custom varies; many tip on pre-tax subtotal. Choose what matches local norms.

How to handle coupons?

Often tip on what the meal would have cost before discount—your choice.

Split uneven orders?

Divide shared items manually or split evenly—this tool assumes equal split.
