How to use

  1. Select daily, weekly, or monthly to match how you are thinking about the numbers.
  2. Enter every revenue line you use in your operation, then enter discounts and refunds as a positive total (they reduce revenue).
  3. Enter variable costs (food, packaging, delivery fees) and fixed-style costs (labor, rent, utilities, marketing, miscellaneous).
  4. Watch the result cards update as you type. Your entries auto-save in this browser unless you clear them.

Formulas used

  • Total revenue = food + beverage + delivery + other sales − discounts/refunds
  • Total variable costs = food cost + packaging + delivery fees
  • Total fixed costs = labor + rent + utilities + marketing + miscellaneous
  • Total expenses = variable costs + fixed costs
  • Gross profit = total revenue − total variable costs
  • Net profit = total revenue − total expenses
  • Profit margin = net profit ÷ total revenue (0% if revenue is 0)
  • Food cost % = food cost ÷ total revenue (0% if revenue is 0)
  • Labor cost % = labor cost ÷ total revenue (0% if revenue is 0)
  • Break-even revenue target = total expenses (revenue needed for net profit ≈ 0 with the same cost structure)
  • Status: profit if net > 0, loss if net < 0, break-even if net = 0

Frequently asked questions

Does this replace my accountant or POS reports?
No. It is a quick planning and teaching view. Always reconcile with your point of sale, bank feeds, and professional advice.
Why do percentages show 0% when revenue is 0?
Percentages divide by revenue. The tool avoids division by zero and shows 0% so you never see NaN or Infinity on screen.
Can I use this for a food truck or café?
Yes. The line items are generic enough for most small food operations; rename categories mentally if your chart of accounts differs.
Where is data stored?
In your browser’s localStorage for this site origin. Clearing site data or using another device starts fresh.
