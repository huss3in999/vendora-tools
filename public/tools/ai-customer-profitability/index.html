What this tool does

The AI Customer Profitability Calculator estimates customer profit from revenue minus direct service and delivery costs. If you add time spent and an hourly value, it also estimates time-based profit.

Why this matters

Some customers look “busy” but are unprofitable after rework, support, and delivery. Knowing which customers are profitable helps you improve pricing, set boundaries, and focus on better-fit customers.

How to use it

  1. Enter revenue, service cost, and delivery cost.
  2. Optionally add time spent and an hourly value to include time cost.
  3. Click Calculate to see profit and short AI actions.

Example

A customer pays $500. Service cost is $180 and delivery cost is $25. Profit (no time cost) is $295. If you spent 3.5 hours and value your time at $35/hour, time cost is $122.50 and profit with time cost becomes $172.50.

FAQs

Do I have to include time?

No. Time is optional. If you leave hourly value blank, the tool will still compute profit from direct costs.

Does AI change my numbers?

No. JavaScript calculates profit locally. AI only receives a short summary of the computed results and returns short suggestions.

Is any customer data stored?

No. The tool does not store customer data. Only a compact summary may be sent to AI for recommendations.
