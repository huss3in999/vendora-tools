      What this tool does

      This AI profit leak finder is a free small business profit tool for quick business profit analysis. You enter one month of revenue and major expense lines. The page calculates totals, estimated profit, margin, and expense ratio, then applies simple rules to find business expense problems such as rent or payroll taking too much of sales. After that, a single AI pass adds a short summary and three actions to help you improve profit margin—written in plain language, not spreadsheets.

      How to use this AI Profit Leak Finder

      Use recent, realistic monthly figures. Start with revenue, then add cost of goods or product cost, staff, rent, utilities, marketing, and anything else material. Pick a business type if you want context for the AI. Click Analyze Business to see your snapshot and warnings, then read AI Profit Tips. Use Copy Summary to paste results into notes or share with a partner. Reset clears the form and results.

      Why businesses lose profit

      Sales can look healthy while margin disappears. Common patterns include product or food cost creeping up, labor scheduled ahead of demand, fixed rent that is high for the revenue base, utilities and subscriptions stacking up, and marketing that is either too weak to drive new sales or too expensive for the return. This tool does not diagnose tax or legal issues; it helps you see where the numbers are tight so you can investigate operations and pricing.

      How the result is calculated

      Total expenses is the sum of the expense fields you entered. Estimated profit is monthly revenue minus total expenses. Profit margin % is estimated profit divided by revenue, times 100. Expense ratio % is total expenses divided by revenue, times 100. Warnings use fixed thresholds—for example, staff cost above 35% of revenue or rent above 10% of revenue—to flag areas that often deserve a closer look.

      Why the AI suggestions can help

      The math tells you what the ratios are; AI turns that into a short narrative and prioritized actions in everyday business language. It does not replace a bookkeeper or advisor. It is useful when you want a fast second opinion on where to focus after you see the snapshot and warnings.

      Example scenario

      A small cafe enters monthly revenue of $42,000, product cost $14,500, staff $16,000, rent $4,200, utilities $900, marketing $800, and other $1,200. Total expenses are $37,600, estimated profit is $4,400, profit margin about 10.5%, and expense ratio about 89.5%. Warnings might flag payroll share and tight margin. AI Profit Tips could suggest reviewing labor scheduling, renegotiating a supplier line, and testing a small price adjustment on high-volume items—always validated against your real operations.

      Frequently asked questions

      What is an AI profit leak finder?

      It is a small business profit tool that combines local business profit analysis with short AI suggestions so you can find business expense problems faster than staring at a blank spreadsheet.

      How is profit margin calculated here?

      Estimated profit is revenue minus entered expenses. Profit margin percent is that profit divided by revenue. All of that runs in your browser.

      Does the AI calculate my totals?

      No. JavaScript calculates totals, margin, and expense ratio, and builds the warning list. AI only receives a compact text summary of those results to return brief tips.

      Is this only for restaurants?

      No. You can use it for retail, salons, services, and other types. The optional business type helps tailor the AI wording.

      Will this fix my business finances?

      It is an educational starting point. Use it with accurate books, professional advice where needed, and your own judgment.
