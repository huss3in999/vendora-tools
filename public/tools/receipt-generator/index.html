What this tool does

The Vendora receipt generator builds a print-ready payment receipt entirely in your browser: you enter merchant identity, receipt metadata, line items with quantity and price, optional percentage or fixed discounts, tax handling, and how the customer paid. A live preview mirrors thermal-style or counter-style layouts so you catch typos before paper or PDF leaves the shop. It is built for speed when a card reader does not produce a branded slip, when you need a duplicate for catering drop-offs, or when a pop-up stall needs something more professional than a notebook scribble. Nothing is uploaded to a server for the core flow—your numbers stay on the device while you work.

Who should use it

Counter-service restaurants, espresso bars, bakeries, boutique retail, mobile beauty, and weekend markets all reach for a receipt maker when customers ask for proof of purchase. Bookkeepers reconciling cash drawers appreciate consistent line math, while freelancers issuing simple sales receipts can avoid opening a full accounting suite. Food trucks and tasting rooms use it after off-POS card transactions, and small hotels or guesthouses can document incidental charges. Pair it with the invoice generator when you need a formal bill first and a payment receipt after funds clear.

How to use it

Start with business name, address, and tax ID fields if your jurisdiction expects them on customer copies. Set receipt number and date, choose payment method, and add optional cashier or customer labels when internal controls require names on the slip. Add each product row with quantity, unit price, and let subtotal roll forward; apply discount and tax rules that match your register policy. Review the preview pane for alignment and wording, adjust footer notes for return policy or gratitude line, then print or use the browser’s print-to-PDF. Re-run whenever you void a line—keeping the preview accurate avoids disputes at the door.

Example

A cafe sells two pastries at $4.50, one sandwich at $11.00, and a latte at $5.25. Subtotal hits $25.25 before a 10% staff discount on the sandwich only and 8.5% sales tax on eligible items. The generator shows each line, the discounted sandwich row, tax breakdown, and grand total with “Card” as tender. The footer reads “Thank you—retain for warranty” so the customer has a clean artifact for an office expense report. That same layout scales to six-line catering pickups by duplicating rows without reformatting the template.

Receipt Generator FAQ

What is a receipt generator?

A receipt generator helps you create a printable receipt with business details, item lines, taxes, discounts, and payment information.

Can I use this as a restaurant receipt generator?

Yes. This tool works well for restaurants, cafes, food trucks, and other food businesses.

Can I print the receipt after generating it?

Yes. The tool should include a print action so users can print the final receipt easily.

Can I add tax and discounts?

Yes. The receipt generator should support tax and discount calculations before showing the final total.
