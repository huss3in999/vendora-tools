Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about how restaurant calculators fit pricing and profit planning.

What are restaurant calculators used for?

Restaurant calculators help owners check food cost, set menu prices, and estimate delivery commission so they can make better daily pricing and margin decisions.

Which calculator should I use first?

Start with the food cost calculator if you need recipe and portion costs, use the menu price calculator when you are setting selling prices, and use the delivery commission calculator when you want to measure marketplace fees.

Are these calculators useful for cafes and food trucks?

Yes. These Vendora calculators are useful for restaurants, cafes, food trucks, takeaways, and other small food businesses that need practical pricing and profit checks.
