Privacy Policy

This page explains, in general terms, how Vendora may handle website usage data, contact requests, and basic information shared through the website.

Information We May Collect

Vendora may collect basic website usage information, information submitted through forms or contact requests, and limited technical data that helps the website function properly. The exact information collected can depend on the page, tool, or service being used.

How Information May Be Used

Information may be used to respond to questions, improve the website, understand how visitors use pages and tools, and support restaurant owners who contact Vendora about tools, ordering flows, pricing, or operations questions.

Cookies and Basic Analytics

Vendora may use cookies, analytics, or similar technologies to understand traffic, page usage, and general website performance. These tools may help identify which pages or tools are most useful to visitors.

Third-Party Services

Some website features may rely on third-party services, such as analytics tools, hosted assets, or embedded services. Those services may process limited technical information according to their own policies.

Contact About Privacy

If you have a privacy-related question about Vendora, you can use the contact page to reach out. Additional privacy contact details may be added later if needed.
